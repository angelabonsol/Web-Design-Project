Web Porfolio: Graphics

Evaluation of web graphics

The website I chose: Apple

Referring to the section in the handout titled Purpose of graphics in web design,
do you feel the graphics on the page you selected serve a purpose?
If so, what is it? If not, why not?

The graphics on the page I selected serve a purpose because its purpose was to advertise their technological products. Thus, displaying very technical graphics support the aesthetic appeal.

Referring to the section in the handout title Guidelines for effective use of web graphics,
do you feel that the graphics on the page meet each guideline? If not, which guidelines
do they not meet?

The graphics on the page meets some aspects of the guideline. I believe the website did not meet guideline number 3, because there was too many graphics going on at a time, displaying more than one feature of their products, as you scroll down.

The file format most appropriate for putting that image on a webpage is jpeg.
